What is Simple Art Club?

The club for simple art connoisseurs.

What can this do?

Create your own Art NFTs to display, share, and earn.

Discord?

Yes! Join our discord!

Supply information

There is a maximum amount of Simple Art Pixel NFTs to be created, up to 3333.

Creation Donation Cost

When creating, a donation amount is applied to help further development.

Creator Royalty

All creators earn 6% royalty forever.

Category Royalty

Categories created for a community or DAO earn 6% royalty forever from their category.

Simple Art Club Royalty

Simple Art Club earns 3% royalty to help sustain development.
